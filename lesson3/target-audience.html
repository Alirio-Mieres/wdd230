Target Audience

The website's audience is people between the ages of 20 and 60. Young people who want to go out with their friends, middle-aged people who want to go out with their family on weekends, visitors from other nearby states to spend vacations.

Persona 1

Thomas: a 24 year old young man about to graduate from college, he is very busy with his homework during the week. He has the internet all the time with which he checks websites in his spare time. On weekends he takes the opportunity to go out with his friends in outdoor activities.

Persona 2

Luis: he is a 35-year-old hardworking man, he works in an office as an administrative assistant. He has broadband internet access. He jogs very early in the morning before going to work. He has 5 children of which 3 are already teenagers and 2 are children. His wife encourages him to do outdoor recreational activities during the week as a family.
